Our story

Food built around your goals, not around convenience.

The Fitletic Kitchen started with a simple frustration — eating well in Mumbai either meant spending hours cooking, paying a fortune at health cafes, or settling for tasteless meal preps.

We built TFK to close that gap. Every meal we make is designed to taste like something you'd cook yourself on a good Sunday — except we do it every single day, for you.

Recipes rotated weekly to prevent meal fatigue
